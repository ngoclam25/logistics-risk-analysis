# Logistics Service Quality & Customer Risk Analysis
Logistics supply chain analysis (2022-2025) processing >110K records using R and Tableau. Developed a Customer Risk Scoring framework and an interactive Revenue vs Risk matrix. Identified top 10 underperforming routes with >70% delay rates, driving data-backed insights to protect a $0.23B revenue stream.

## Overview
This project analyzes logistics service quality and customer risk using statistical analysis and data visualization techniques.

## Key Insights & Business Recommendations

Based on the interactive dashboard and R analysis, here are the strategic takeaways:

* **Service Quality:** The overall On-Time Delivery (OTD) rate is at an alarmingly low level of **28.25%**, and this rate has not improved over the 3-year period from 2022 to 2024, signaling a systemic operational issue that poses a massive risk for retaining high-value customers.

* **High-Risk Route Identification:** Underperforming operational routes have delay rates **exceeding 70%**. Service quality for these routes should be reviewed.

* **Revenue at Risk:** The **Food/Beverage** and **Consumer Goods** segments have the highest severe delay rates, especially **Food/Beverage** which has a very high total revenue. Immediate processing priority is required (e.g., prioritizing the release of goods detained at warehouses) to secure this **0.23 billion dollar** revenue stream.
